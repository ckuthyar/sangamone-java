NEW DELHI All-rounder Ravindra Jadeja smashed 36 runs in a 37-run final over, which included five huge sixes, as he guided Chennai Super Kings (CSK) to 191 for four against Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) in the Indian Premier League (IPL) match in Mumbai on Sunday.
Jadeja (3/13) later grabbed three big wickets in an outstanding all-round display as CSK crushed RCB by 69 runs.
The left-handed batsman got a life on nought by Dan Christian off Washington Sundar in the 15th over.
